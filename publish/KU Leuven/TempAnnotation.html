1 Standard Temporal Event Annotaiton contains three main components:

Timex, Event and Links.

  • Timex is a time anchor that represent a specific time.
  • Event is usually an action
  • Links represent the relations between Timexes and Events (T-E, T-T, E-E).

Except the main components, Signal and Make_instance are introduced into the framework, where signal represent the explicit relations between two entities (Timex or Event), make_instance represent the repeatition of action in different times.

1.1 Timex

The recognition of Timex includes the recognition of text span, the classification of type and the parse of text to standard time/date.

1.3 Links

Three types of links are defined: TLink, SLink, ALink.

  • TLink: before, after, include, included, during, simultaneous, etc.
  • SLink: describe the modal, factive, evidential links. (e.g. forget to do somthing)
  • ALink: aspectual link, like started, finished, stopped, kept, resumed.
